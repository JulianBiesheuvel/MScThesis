Column definitions
ar    : mass-balance year
d1    : date of field visit in the fall of the previous year
d2    : date of field visit in the spring
d3    : date of field visit in the fall of the current year
x     : x coordinate in spring (ISN93)
y     : y coordinate in spring (ISN93)
h     : elevation in spring (m a.s.l.)
dw    : depth to the previous summer surface in the spring (d2) (m)
rhow  : average density of winter snowpack, that is density of the snowpack above the previous summer surface in the spring (d2) (kg/m³)
imw   : melting of glacier ice during winter (below the spring snow line, NULL otherwise, usually NULL) (m w.e.)
ds    : depth to the previous summer surface in the fall (d3) (m)
rhos  : average density of the snowpack above the previous summer surface in the fall (d3) (kg/m³)
ims   : melting of glacier ice and snow/firn from earlier years during summer (below the ELA, NULL otherwise) (m w.e.)
nswe  : total water equivalent of the new snow above the current summer surface in the fall (m w.e.)
bw_str    : stratigraphic winter balance (m w.e.)
bs_str    : stratigraphic summer balance (m w.e.)
ba_str    : stratigraphic annual balance (m w.e.)
bw_fld    : floating-date winter balance (m w.e.)
bs_fld    : floating-date summer balance (m w.e.)
ba_fld    : floating-date annual balance (m w.e.)
